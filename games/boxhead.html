BOXHEAD 2PLAY ROOMS

MAKE SURE TO ENABLE FLASH!

HOW TO PLAY 

P1: MOVE WITH ↑ → ← ↓
P2: MOVE WITH W A S D
------
Shoot:
Player 1: (Single Player) Space
Player 1: (Multi Player): /
Player 2: Space
------
WEAPONS:
Single Player: 0 through 9
Player 1 (Multi Player): . and , keys
Player 2: Q and E
------
Objective
Kill or be killed. Collect as many weapons and powerups along the way and always, always keep that multiplier up!
------
Getting more guns
You earn more weapons, or upgrades for your current weapons by increasing your multiplier. Every kill will increase it by 1. The multiplier will go back down over time, and this speeds up depending on how high your multiplier is.
